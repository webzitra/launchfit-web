Questions

Frequently asked

How does it work? Is there coding involved?

Zero coding. You answer a few questions about your business, pick a style you like, and our AI builds your entire website. You review it, make changes in plain English, and publish. The whole process takes about 5 minutes.

Do I really own the website?

Yes. You get the complete source code hosted on your own domain. There's no proprietary platform locking you in. If you ever want to leave, you take everything with you — code, content, domain, all of it.

How do I update my site after launch?

With the AI editor (included in Pro and Premium, or $49/mo add-on). Just describe what you want: "Add a new training program called Summer Shred for $199" and the AI updates your site in seconds. No technical knowledge needed.

What makes this different from Wix or Squarespace?

Speed, quality, and ownership. Wix gives you a slow website on their platform that you don't own. We give you a 99+ PageSpeed site with clean code you fully own. Plus, it's built specifically for fitness professionals — not a generic template.

What if I need changes to the design?

Pro and Premium plans include revision rounds before launch. After launch, you can make unlimited changes through the AI editor. Need something more complex? Our team is available for custom work.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

If you're not happy with your website before it goes live, we'll rework it until you are or refund your payment. We want you to love your site as much as your clients love your coaching.
