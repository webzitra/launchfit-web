FAQ

Common questions

How can you deliver in 24 hours?

We combine AI-powered tools with expert designers. Our system generates a custom website from your brief, which our team then refines and polishes. The result: agency-quality work at startup speed.

Do I own my website?

Yes, 100%. You get the full source code, hosted on your own domain. No lock-in, no proprietary platforms. If you ever want to move, you take everything with you.

Can I edit my website myself?

With our optional $49/mo plan, you get access to an AI-powered editor. Just describe what you want to change in plain English, and the AI updates your site. No coding needed.

What if I don't like the design?

We offer revisions before going live. If you're not happy, we'll rework it until you are. Pro and Premium plans include dedicated revision rounds.

Do I need to provide photos?

It helps! But if you don't have professional photos yet, we can use high-quality stock images matched to your brand. You can always swap them later.

Is there a monthly fee?

The website itself is a one-time payment. Hosting and domain cost about $10–15/year. The optional $49/mo plan adds AI editing, priority support, and regular updates.
